Looking period specific?

Triassic

The Triassic Period (252–201 million years ago) was when dinosaurs first appeared. Earth was hot and dry with vast deserts, but life was bouncing back after the biggest mass extinction ever.

Jurassic

The Jurassic Period (201–145 million years ago) was a warm, tropical age ruled by giants like Brachiosaurus and Stegosaurus. Massive conifer forests and shallow seas made it a dino paradise!

Cretaceous

The Cretaceous Period (145–66 million years ago) was the final era of the dinosaurs, featuring lush forests and flowering plants. It ended with the famous asteroid impact that wiped out most dinos—except for their bird relatives!
